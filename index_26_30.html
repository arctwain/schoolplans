Grammar

  • A preposition shows how nouns and pronouns relate to the other words in a sentence. They often show where something is or when something happened.
  • Example: We skated on the icy lake.
  • The preposition and the noun or pronoun after it make up a prepositional phrase.
  • The prepositional phrase in the sentence above is "on the icy lake".
  • Complete Brain Quest, pp. 32-3 – Over Under; Where's Furious?
