Math

  • Remember: The number being divided is called the dividend. The number being divided into it is called the divisor. The answer to a division problem is called the quotient. The quotient may include a remainder.
  • Brain Quest, p. 149: Divide and Conquer
  • Look at the example: The divisor is 8. The dividend is 57. First, set up the problem: 57 ÷ 8
  • Next figure out the the following: What is the highest number that you can multiply by 8 without going over 57? Since 7 x 8 = 56, we write down 7.
  • Finally, the difference between 57 and 56 is 1, and this is our remainder. Notice how they write 7 r1 to show the answer and remainder.
