Writing

  • Learn how to write a persuasive (also called argument) essay on this site.
  • You will be writing an essay, which will be due at the end of next week. First, choose a topic from the following:
    • Should the voting age be lowered to thirteen?
    • Is fashion important?
    • Are we too dependent on computers?
    • Should all textbooks be replaced by notebook computers?
    • To what extent is the use of animals in scientific research acceptable?
  • Pick whether you will argue for or against the question, and write your essay statement at the center of the top line of a sheet of paper. For example, you may choose to write "The voting age should be lowered to thirteen."
  • Draw a line going down the middle of your page.
  • On the left side, write three different reasons why your argument is correct (pros).
  • On the right side, write two different reasons to support the opposing point of view (cons).
